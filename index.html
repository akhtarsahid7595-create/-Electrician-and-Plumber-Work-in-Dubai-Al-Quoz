Maintenance Solutions for Dubai Homes

Handling Dubai Heat

The extreme summer temperatures in Al Quoz and across Dubai put immense pressure on electrical systems and water heaters. Our technicians are specialized in heavy-duty wiring and AC power troubleshooting to prevent fire hazards and power trips during peak heat months.

Advanced Leak Detection

Water is a precious resource in the UAE. Tiny leaks in your plumbing can lead to massive DEWA bills and structural damage. We use non-invasive acoustic leak detection technology to find hidden pipe bursts in Al Quoz warehouses and residential villas without breaking your walls.

DEWA Standards Compliance

All our electrical work follows strict DEWA (Dubai Electricity & Water Authority) regulations. From DB board dressing to proper earthing, we ensure your property passes safety inspections and operates at maximum efficiency.
